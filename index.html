Welcome to Viral Lab!

We work hard, dream big, and enjoy life while developing techniques to analyze online behaviors to improve individual well-being and address societal problems. We leverage online and offline data to gain insight on human behaviors, analyze technological systems to mitigate threats against online conversations by developing techniques in machine learning, network science, and computational social sciences.

The Viral Lab is led by Onur Varol, an assistant professor at the Sabanci University Computer Science Program, with an affiliation with Center of Excellence in Data Anlytics.
